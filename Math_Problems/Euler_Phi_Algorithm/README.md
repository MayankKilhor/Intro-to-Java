# Euler's phi Algorithm
## Given a number, N. Find the sum of its natural co-prime till N.



Sample Input1:

10

Sample Output1:

20

Explanation:

The co-primes of 10 are 1, 3, 7 and 9. Their sum will be 20. 



Sample Input2:

8

Sample Output2:

16

Explanation:

The co-primes of 8 are 1, 3, 5 and 7. Their sum will be 16.
